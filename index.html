Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026 · AI TRY (“we”, “us”)

1. Scope

This Policy describes how we process personal data when you use authentication, virtual try-on, optional ads, subscriptions, weather context, and cloud features.

2. Data we process

  • Account data: user ID, email, authentication metadata.
  • Profile data: body-related parameters, style preferences, language, city or region where you provide it, subscription tier, credits.
  • User media: profile photo, garment photos, try-on results, optional label photos.
  • Billing and usage: ad reward events, credit operations, subscription/payment-related events.
  • Location and weather: source (e.g. GPS, IP, manual), city, weather summary where used.
  • Technical data: logs and operational data for reliability and security.

3. Service providers

We use providers such as Supabase (auth, database, storage), Google Gemini (AI processing), Google AdMob (rewarded ads), Stripe (payments, when enabled), and weather/geolocation services. They process data under their own terms.

4. Purposes

  • Run the app and sync your data.
  • Generate try-on and related features.
  • Manage credits, ads, and subscriptions.
  • Localise and personalise the experience.
  • Build aggregated, non-identifying statistics about apparel categories, preferences, and usage for industry insight products.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

5. Industry insights — important

We do not sell your personal data. We do not give fashion retailers, marketplaces, or similar partners your name, email, account identifiers, photos, or other information in a form that identifies you as an individual.

We may license or share aggregated and de-identified statistics (for example shares of categories, colours, brands, or broad demographic bands such as age range or country region) where the output is not intended to identify any person.

6. Legal bases (EU / UK)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on contract (core features), consent where required (including certain advertising), legitimate interests (security, reliability, and where applicable preparation of aggregated industry statistics that do not identify individuals), and legal obligation where applicable.

7. International transfers

Data may be processed outside your country on provider infrastructure. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards.

8. Retention and deletion

We keep personal data while your account is active and as needed for law, security, or disputes. You can request account deletion in the app. After deletion, aggregated statistics that cannot be linked to you may be retained.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object, portability, and to withdraw consent. US state laws may provide additional rights. You may contact a supervisory authority where applicable.

10. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the higher minimum age in your region).

11. Changes

We may update this Policy and change the “Last updated” date above.

Account deletion

App only. AI TRY is available only as a mobile application. There is no web login—accounts are used inside the app.

Steps to delete your account and personal data:

  1. Open the AI TRY app and sign in.
  2. Open your Profile screen.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This removes your account, profile-linked data, and associated files (e.g. photos and try-on results) according to our backend. Completion may take a short time.

If you cannot use the app, email us (see Contact below) with subject line Account deletion and, if possible, the email address on your account.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-25 · AI TRY (“we”, “our”, “us”)

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how AI TRY (“we”, “our”, “us”) processes personal data when you use authentication, virtual try-on, ads, subscriptions, weather context, and cloud storage features.

This Policy is intended for users in the EU/EEA, UK, US, and other jurisdictions with equal or less strict privacy requirements.

2. Data We Process

  • Account data: user ID, email address, authentication provider metadata.
  • Profile data: body-related parameters, style preferences, app language, city, subscription tier, and credits balance.
  • User media: profile photo, garment photos, virtual try-on results, and optional label photos.
  • Usage and billing events: rewarded ad events, credit operations, and subscription/payment status events.
  • Location and weather context: location source (GPS/IP/manual), city, and weather summary.
  • Technical and diagnostic data: logs and operational events needed for service reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

3. Sources of Data

  • Directly from you (for example profile inputs and uploaded photos).
  • Automatically from app/device usage (for example diagnostics and service events).
  • From integrated providers (for example authentication, billing, ads, weather and geolocation services).

4. Services We Use (Processors / Third Parties)

  • Supabase: authentication, database, storage, and RPC.
  • Google Gemini API: AI image analysis and virtual try-on generation.
  • Google AdMob: rewarded ads.
  • Stripe: checkout and payment/subscription processing (when enabled).
  • OpenWeather and fallback geolocation providers: weather and location context.

These providers process data under their own privacy terms and policies.

5. Purposes of Processing

  • Provide account access and sync app data.
  • Generate virtual try-on outputs and styling features.
  • Manage credits, rewarded ads, and subscriptions.
  • Localize and personalize user experience.
  • Produce aggregated, non-identifying statistics about apparel categories, preferences, and in-app usage for industry insight products (see section 9).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse/fraud, and troubleshoot incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

6. Legal Bases (EU/UK)

Where GDPR / UK GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract (core app functionality).
  • Consent (where required, including certain advertising/marketing processing).
  • Legitimate interests (service security, reliability, abuse prevention, and—where applicable—preparing aggregated industry statistics that do not identify individuals).
  • Legal obligations (where applicable).

7. Ads and Consent

We use AdMob for rewarded ads. Where required by law (including parts of EU/EEA/UK), consent is requested and can be updated via privacy options available in the app experience or platform controls.

8. Data Sharing and International Transfers

We may share relevant data with service providers listed above to deliver app functionality, payments, AI processing, ads, and infrastructure operations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not provide your name, email address, account identifiers, photos, or other content in a form that identifies you to fashion retailers, marketplaces, or similar partners for their own marketing or profiling of you as an individual.

We may license or share aggregated and de-identified statistics about clothing categories, brands, colours, general demographics (for example age range or country region in summary form), and usage patterns, where those outputs are designed so they do not reasonably identify any person. Partners in the fashion and retail sector may receive only such statistical reports or datasets—not your account or gallery.

Depending on provider infrastructure, personal data may be processed outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

9. Account deletion and retention

How to delete your account. AI TRY is available only as a mobile application for Android (and any other platforms we publish). There is no web login or browser version of the app—accounts are created and used inside the app only.

To delete your account and associated personal data:

  1. Open the AI TRY app and sign in.
  2. Open your Profile screen.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm when prompted.

This triggers removal of your account and profile-linked records and associated files (such as photos and try-on results) in line with our backend configuration. Processing may take a short time to complete across systems.

If you cannot use the app (for example you uninstalled it or lost access to your device), you may contact us by email (see section 14). Include “Account deletion” in the subject line and, if possible, the email address linked to your account so we can verify your request.

Retention. We retain personal data while your account is active unless a longer retention period is required by law, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or security obligations.

After account deletion, industry statistics that are already aggregated and do not identify individuals may be retained where they cannot be linked back to your account.

Certain internal and external analytics outputs are produced only in anonymized and/or aggregated form and are not intended to identify an individual user.

10. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including authentication controls, access restrictions, and storage/database permissions. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete personal data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority/regulator.

US State Privacy Rights: where applicable under US state privacy laws, you may also have rights to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain data uses/sharing.

12. Children

AI TRY is not directed to children under 13 (or under the higher minimum age required by local law). If you believe a child provided personal data unlawfully, contact us for deletion.

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will publish the updated version and revise the “Last updated” date.
